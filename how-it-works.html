How RailNav Works

1

Select Your Station

Choose your railway station from the available station list. Each station includes updated navigation details and facility information. This ensures you get accurate guidance before and during your journey.

2

View Available Facilities

Instantly access locations of platforms, washrooms, lifts, exits, waiting halls, ticket counters, and parking areas. All facilities are clearly organized for easy understanding. This helps passengers save time and avoid unnecessary confusion.

3

Use Smart Navigation

Follow simple and clear step-by-step directions inside the station. RailNav provides structured guidance to reach your destination quickly. The system reduces stress and makes station navigation smooth and efficient.

4

Access Emergency Support

Get immediate access to important emergency contacts and helplines. Information for Railway Police, medical services, and safety support is always available. Your safety and security remain our highest priority at all times.
